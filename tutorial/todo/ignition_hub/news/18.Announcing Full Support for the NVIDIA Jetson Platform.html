Announcing Full Support for the NVIDIA Jetson Platform

xInfer is now the definitive high-performance inference toolkit for robotics, drones, and the entire edge AI ecosystem.

Published: March 26, 2026 | By: The Ignition AI Team

The future of AI is at the edge. From autonomous drones to industrial robots, the world's most innovative products are being built on embedded, power-constrained hardware. At the heart of this revolution is the NVIDIA Jetson platform.

Today, we are thrilled to announce that the entire `xInfer` ecosystem, including the `Ignition Hub`, now offers **first-class, production-ready support for the NVIDIA Jetson family**, including the Jetson AGX Orin, Orin Nano, and Xavier NX.

Performance Where It Matters Most

On a desktop GPU, performance is a luxury. On a Jetson, **performance-per-watt is everything**. A more efficient inference engine means longer drone flight times, smaller battery packs, and the ability to run more complex AI models on cheaper hardware.

Our `xInfer` toolkit is built for this world. Our C++ architecture has minimal overhead, and our ability to build hyper-optimized INT8 TensorRT engines is a game-changer for the power-constrained Jetson platform.

The Ignition Hub for Jetson

The "build barrier" is even more painful on embedded devices. Building a TensorRT engine directly on a Jetson can be slow and can tax the system's limited memory. The Ignition Hub solves this.

Our Hub now contains a massive catalog of pre-built engines specifically compiled and tuned for the Jetson architecture (`sm_87` and `sm_75`). You can now get a hyper-optimized YOLOv8 engine for your Orin Nano with a single command:

# Download the perfect engine for your robot
xinfer::hub::HardwareTarget my_jetson = {"Jetson_Orin_Nano", "10.0.1", "INT8"};
auto detector = xinfer::zoo::vision::Detector("yolov8n-coco", my_jetson);
